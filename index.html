About Me

I am currently serving as the Director of Product for Poly Platform, a "Smart-City" civic engagement app that allows municipalities to digitize their constituent services and improve the way the communicate with their residents. We hope to ultimately revolutionize the way our democracy responds to the needs of its people. I am highly interested in emerging technology, particularly new applications for the distributed ledger technology utilized by blockchain as well as generative AI. Prior to my MBA at Georgetown, I worked as a Research Analyst at the Boston Consulting Group (BCG) specializing in the Energy practice area. I have extensive experience in market analysis using a variety of proprietary databases such as:

  • Bloomberg Terminal
  • S&P Capital IQ
  • Thomson Reuters Eikon
  • IHS Markit
  • Rystad Oil and Gas Database
I have also previously worked on Capitol Hill as a congressional legislative intern at the U.S. House of Representatives and a policy research intern for the Cato Institute. In my free time, I enjoy listening to history podcasts and watching UFC.

Poly Platform

Poly Platform is a smart-city platform that consolidates many third-party tools onto one easy-to-use and cost-effective platform. Poly Platform aims to improve civic engagement, boost data-driven decisions within city administration, and improve municipal efficiencies. Using our platform, municipalities can promote town events and local businesses while using our data-driven insights to create their own economic development plans. In addition, this platform allows towns to promote awareness of local initiatives and integrate active resident feedback into their town’s development process.

  • I have been the Director of Product at Poly Platform since March 2023
  • Since I joined Poly Platform has rasied $200k in Venture Capital funding and expanded from 14 to 25 municipal customers
  • As the Director of Product, my goal is to build out key features that will help revolutionize digital government such as AI assistants and Blockchain-based digital identity
